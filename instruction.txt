Introduction of how to interact to the web page
1.	  When cursor reaches to menu, it’s color will be changed. If you clicked on it, you will go to the 
related section of the page. 
2.	When browser become smaller, menu and pictures will be flexible depending on size of it.
3.	When you make events of mouseenter, mouseleave and click on “Skills” section, background color 
will be changed. It was made using Jquery.
